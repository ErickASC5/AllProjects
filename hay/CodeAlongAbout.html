Type-Down

Whenever you feel down, type down your story.

This is Code-Along by Diego ,Alyeard and Erick. Now you may ask what is Code-Along. Well Code-Along is a website that teaches everyone how to code. While, there are other programs like this, our product is unique. All other coding programs require you to either pay, have knownlege of coding or even have certain skill-sets. We offer free services to anyone that is interested in coding. This product will not only help everyone but allow people of color to become more mainstream in the tech industry. They will not have to apply for any programs which may be rigorous or even have to pay for but instead have a learning tool. A tool that can be used anywhere and anytime. Even if you are in an area with no wifi, you can still use it. The main reason for making this tool is to help diversify the tech world. The benifits of diversity are limitless but Ill give you a few examples. Things such as growth mindset are because of a diverse setting. It allows the person to not only think about their problems but become more aware of other problems that do not affect them. It elimates the I-Can't ideology and allows the person to become more confident and tell themselves I CAN! I hope you all enjoy our product and remember that anyone can code, no matter what others say.
